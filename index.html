Frequently Asked Questions

Will the alarms work if I don't have WiFi? ▼

Absolutely. Scriptomi is built to be a standalone offline app. All medication reminders are scheduled locally on your device's system.

Where is my prescription data stored? ▼

Everything is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

Can I manage medications for my kids? ▼

Yes! You can create multiple profiles. Each profile has its own medication list, history, and prescription gallery.

Is Scriptomi available for iPhone/iOS? ▼

Currently, Scriptomi is only available for Android devices. We're working hard on an iOS version and will announce when it's ready!
