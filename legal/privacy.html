Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Summary

We believe health data should be private and tools should be affordable. Scriptomi is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal health data to our servers.

What data is stored

All medication lists, alarms, photos of prescriptions, and profile information are stored locally on your device.

  • Medication names, doses, schedules
  • Profile information (name, relation, notes)
  • Photos of prescriptions (stored in app storage)

Third-party services

We use the Google Play Store for app distribution. We do not integrate analytics, ads, or third-party trackers in the app.

Backup & export

If you export or backup data from the app, ensure you keep backups secure. Backups created by the user may leave the device if you choose to upload them to cloud storage.

Contact

For questions about privacy, email support@tekibo.in.
